## What is Musubi?

Musubi (結び) means *knot* or *tie* in Japanese — the idea being that the app ties people's schedules together through shared calendars and a clean collaborative interface.

<CardGrid stagger>
  <Card title="Shared Calendars" icon="star">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Calendar Invites" icon="add-document">
    Send and accept invitations to join calendars, with full control over who can see or edit.
  </Card>
